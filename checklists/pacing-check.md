# Pacing Check

## Slide roles

1. Hook
2. What happened
3. How it worked / mechanism
4. Reversal or emotional turn
5. Escalation
6. System meaning
7. Open close

## Pass rules

- one major reveal per slide
- each slide answers one question and opens another
- slide 1 does not contain what + how + who benefits + consequence all at once
- slide 4 or 5 increases stakes
- close does not wrap too neatly

## Auto-fail

- front-loading the story in slide 1
- repetitive slides with no new information
- all tension resolved before final slide
